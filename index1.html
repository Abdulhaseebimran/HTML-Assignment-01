UEFA Conference League Conference

The UEFA Conference League Conference is an annual event for football club executives, managers, and coaches to discuss the latest trends and developments in the UEFA Conference League. Participants will have the opportunity to attend talks, workshops, and networking sessions with other industry professionals.

he conference will take place on September 1-3, 2023 at the Estádio do Dragão in Porto, Portugal.

Conference Schedule

  • September 1: Registration, Keynote Speech, Sessions on Financial Fair Play
  • September 2: Sessions on Club Development, Roundtable Discussions, Social Events
  • September 3: Workshops on Player Recruitment, Closing Remarks
